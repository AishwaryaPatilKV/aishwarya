About me

I have hands-on experience applying statistical and optimisation techniques to real-world problems, including insurance reserving, predictive modelling, and cost optimisation. I am particularly interested in actuarial science, data-driven risk analysis, and quantitative roles where mathematical rigour and analytical thinking are central to decision-making.
